Pain reports mapped across 18 anatomical regions. See where your team hurts at a glance.

▤

Team Trends

Readiness, energy, and effort tracked over time. Understand how training load affects your roster.

★

AI Assistant

Ask natural language questions and get instant, data-backed answers. No spreadsheets required.

⚙

Workout Logging

✎

Custom Survey Templates

Build bespoke survey flows for any scenario—pre-game, post-practice, weekly wellness, rehab updates. Fully configurable question types with Likert scales, numeric inputs, and free text.
