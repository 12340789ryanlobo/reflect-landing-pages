25 Iterations of Reflect

A rapid prototyping exercise exploring radically different design directions for a single product — a fitness and rehab tracking app for coaches and athletes.

The Design Story

This project compresses the full design exploration process into 25 landing pages. Each version reimagines the same product — Reflect — through a completely different visual lens. The goal isn't to find one "right" answer immediately, but to explore as widely as possible and discover what works through iteration.

v1 – v10: Go Wide

The first ten versions are radical experiments. Each one inhabits a completely different visual world — from clean SaaS minimalism to Y2K nostalgia, from brutalist anti-design to editorial warmth. The goal here is divergent thinking: push in every direction and see what resonates.

v11 – v20: Explore Further

The second set continues exploring new territories: athletic energy, glassmorphism, healthcare credibility, luxury aesthetics, split-screen layouts, cyberpunk, organic warmth, and strict Bauhaus geometry. Each version tests a different hypothesis about how Reflect should feel.

v21 – v25: Specialized Directions

The final five versions explore more targeted aesthetics: dashboard-forward data visualization, social fitness community, monochrome constraint, technical blueprint precision, and clean card-based modern design. Version 25 represents the most polished, ship-ready direction.
