Iterating on Reflect

A rapid prototyping exercise: 25 explorations, 10 combinations, and 10 component-driven refinements for a fitness and rehab tracking app.

The Design Story

This project compresses the full design exploration process into three rounds of iteration, narrowing from 25 radically different directions down to one final page.

Round 1 (25 pages): Go Wide

25 radically different explorations — minimal SaaS, brutalist, editorial, Apple minimal, startup gradient, developer docs, dark mode, playful wellness, retro Y2K, bold typography, Nike athletic, glassmorphism, healthcare, luxury sport, duotone, split-screen, newspaper, cyberpunk, organic, Bauhaus, dashboard, Strava social, monochrome, blueprint, and card-based modern.

Round 2 (10 pages): Combine Favorites

Identified the strongest elements from Round 1 — Apple-minimal typography, vibrant gradients, dark premium feel, product demos showing SMS conversations, dashboard data visualization, and discoverable hover interactions — and combined them into 10 focused templates.

Round 2.5 (10 pages + finals): Component-Driven Rebuild

Rebuilt from scratch using Magic UI and shadcn component patterns as the primary design system: DotPattern, BorderBeam, ShineBorder, AnimatedGradientText, iPhone 15 Pro mockups, Safari browser frames, BentoGrids, Marquee testimonials, Ripple effects, and RetroGrid — all translated to pure CSS. Then refined the strongest direction (v10) through three targeted passes based on specific feedback, producing the final page.

Final Choice

The result of 45+ iterations, combining the best elements from every round.
