What Is Reflect

The always-on assistant coach

Reflect automates post-practice and post-competition check-ins through SMS and WhatsApp. Athletes respond to brief surveys covering readiness, energy, effort, and injuries. Coaches receive instant flag alerts and a real-time dashboard tracking team patterns.

No app to install. No friction. Just data that protects your athletes.
