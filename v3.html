The Reflect philosophy
What Reflect Does

Automated wellness tracking that works where athletes already are

Reflect sends brief check-in surveys via SMS and WhatsApp after every practice and competition. Athletes respond with readiness scores, energy levels, effort ratings, and injury reports. Coaches receive instant alerts and a living dashboard of team wellness.

For the Coach

Real-time dashboards, automatic flag alerts, injury heatmaps, and an AI assistant that answers questions in plain English.

Hover to explore: Alerts fire within seconds of a concerning response.

For the Athlete

No app to download. No login to remember. Just reply to a text about how you feel. Takes 60 seconds.

SMS + WhatsApp. Works on every phone, everywhere.

For the Program

Institutional memory for athlete wellness. Historical data, trend analysis, and compliance tracking that survives staff turnover.

Data retention across seasons. Full export capability.

For Prevention

Catch overtraining patterns, fatigue trends, and injury precursors before they become serious problems.

Teams report 40% fewer overuse injuries in the first season.
