The Reflect philosophy
What Reflect Does

Automated wellness tracking that works where athletes already are

Reflect sends brief check-in surveys via SMS and WhatsApp after every practice and competition. Athletes respond with readiness scores, energy levels, effort ratings, and injury reports. Coaches receive instant alerts and a living dashboard of team wellness.

For the Coach

Real-time dashboards, automatic flag alerts, injury heatmaps, and an AI assistant that answers questions about your team in plain English.

For the Athlete

No app to download. No login to remember. Just reply to a text about how you feel. Takes 60 seconds after every session.

For the Program

Institutional memory for athlete wellness. Historical data, trend analysis, and compliance tracking that survives staff turnover.

For Prevention

Catch overtraining patterns, fatigue trends, and injury precursors before they become serious problems that cost athletes their season.

A closer look at the details

Every feature built around a single principle: make it effortless for athletes to report, and effortless for coaches to act.

Feature 01

Automatic Flag Alerts

Instant notifications when athletes report low readiness, injury pain, or high fatigue. The system watches so you don't have to.

Feature 02

Injury Body Heatmap

A visual map of injury reports across 18 anatomical regions. Patterns emerge that spreadsheets can never reveal.

Feature 03

Team Trends Dashboard

Readiness, energy, and effort visualized over time. See how training load affects your team across a full season arc.

Feature 04

AI Coaching Assistant

Natural language queries against your own data. Ask questions, get answers backed by real athlete check-ins.

Feature 05

Per-Player Drill-Down

Individual athlete profiles with full wellness history, injury logs, and trend analysis for deeper one-on-one conversations.

Feature 06

Custom Question Templates

Tailor every check-in to your sport and your philosophy. Add questions that matter to your specific program.
