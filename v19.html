What Is Reflect?

The always-on assistant coach

Reflect automates athlete wellness check-ins so you know how every player on your team feels — physically and mentally — after every practice and competition.

Athletes respond to brief SMS or WhatsApp surveys. No app download required. No friction. Coaches receive real-time alerts, dashboards, and AI-powered insights that make invisible patterns visible.

Works where athletes already are. Check-ins that take 60 seconds. Insights that last a season.

Gentle Tools, Powerful Results

Everything your team needs to thrive

Flag Alerts

Automatic notifications when athletes report low readiness, injury concerns, or high pain. Catch problems early, when they're still seeds.

Body Heatmap

18 anatomical regions visualized across your entire roster. See where your team hurts and watch patterns shift with the seasons.

Team Trends

Readiness, energy, and effort flowing over time. Understand the natural rhythms of your team through a full season.

AI Assistant

Ask questions in plain language. "Who's been fatigued this week?" Get answers rooted in your own data, delivered in seconds.

Workout Logging

Athletes log workouts and rehab through the same channel. Track compliance organically, without extra burden on anyone.

Leaderboards

Player Profiles

Custom Surveys

1
