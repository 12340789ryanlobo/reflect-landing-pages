Features

Everything your program needs

From automated surveys to AI insights, Reflect handles the workflow so you can focus on coaching.

✉

SMS & WhatsApp Check-ins

No app to download. Athletes respond to brief surveys right where they already message. 94% average response rate.

⚠

Automatic Alerts

Instant flags for low readiness, injury concerns, high pain, and unusual patterns. Never miss a warning sign.

■

Team Trends Dashboard

Readiness, energy, and effort over time. Spot how your team responds to training load changes at a glance.

★

AI Coaching Assistant

Ask questions in plain English and get instant answers. "Who needs a rest day?" Data-backed, no spreadsheets.

◉

Injury Heatmap

18 anatomical regions tracked across your whole roster. Visualize team-wide pain patterns in seconds.

✎

Custom Templates

Build question sets for pre-game, post-practice, rehab check-ins, and more. Fully flexible to your program.

How It Works

Live in 5 minutes. No app required.

Four steps to complete visibility into your team's wellness.

01

Add your athletes

Import your roster with names and phone numbers. That is the only setup needed.

02

Schedule check-ins

Set automatic post-practice or post-game survey times. Reflect handles delivery.

03

Athletes respond in 60s

Quick SMS or WhatsApp surveys: readiness, energy, effort, and pain. Done before they leave.

04

You get the full picture

Live dashboard, trending data, automatic alerts, and an AI assistant ready to answer your questions.
