Built for the demands of sport

Six capabilities designed for coaches managing competitive programs.

💬

Automated SMS & WhatsApp Check-ins

Conversational wellness surveys delivered to athletes' phones at the times you set. No app downloads. 94% average response rate. Athletes reply in under 30 seconds with simple taps.

🚩

Smart Flag Alerts

Detect declining sleep, spiking soreness, or missed check-ins — and get alerted before small signals become serious injuries.

🩸

Injury Heatmaps

Visual body maps aggregate pain data across your roster. See where tightness clusters and adjust training proactively.

🤖

AI Coaching Assistant

Ask questions in natural language: "Who should I rest this week?" or "How is the team trending since last tournament?" Reflect synthesizes your data into actionable coaching insights — not just numbers, but recommendations.

📈

Real-Time Dashboards

Readiness, soreness, sleep, and mood — every athlete in one view. Trends update as responses flow in live.

🔒

Athlete Privacy

Athletes control what's shared. Coaches see aggregate wellness, not private details. FERPA and HIPAA aligned.
