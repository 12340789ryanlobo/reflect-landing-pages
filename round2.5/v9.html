🚀 Process

Up and running in 10 minutes

No app downloads. No athlete onboarding. Just add numbers and go.

1
Import Your Roster
Upload a CSV or add athletes manually. Name and phone number — that's all Reflect needs.
2
Schedule Check-Ins
Set when check-ins go out — post-practice, game day, rest day. Customize questions per sport.
3
Athletes Reply via SMS
60-second text conversations. No app to download. Athletes just reply to messages like they already do.
4
Coach Gets Insights
Real-time dashboard with readiness scores, injury heatmaps, flag alerts, and AI-powered recommendations.
