How it works

Meet athletes where they are — their phone

No app downloads. No logins. Reflect sends conversational check-ins via SMS or WhatsApp at the times you set. Athletes respond in 30 seconds with simple taps. You get structured wellness data, instantly on your dashboard.


Morning readiness. Post-practice soreness. Game-day mood. Every check-in builds a longitudinal picture of each athlete — and your team as a whole.
