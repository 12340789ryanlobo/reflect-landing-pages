Meet athletes where they are — their phone.

Reflect sends automated check-ins via SMS or WhatsApp. Athletes respond in under 60 seconds with simple ratings. No app downloads. No logins. No friction.

  • ✓ SMS & WhatsApp — meets athletes on the platforms they already use, every single day
  • ✓ Smart follow-ups — automatic injury probes and trend-aware responses that feel personal

Process

Three steps. Zero friction.

01

Schedule

Set your check-in times and roster. Reflect handles the rest — automated texts go out on your schedule, every practice and game day.

02

Respond

Athletes text back in under 60 seconds. Simple ratings, no app required. Response rates that blow away any survey tool you've tried.

03

Insight

Coaches get real-time dashboards, flag alerts, and AI-powered insights. Spot issues before they become injuries. Make decisions with data.
