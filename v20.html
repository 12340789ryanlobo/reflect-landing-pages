What Is Reflect?

The Always-On Assistant Coach

Reflect automates post-practice and post-competition check-ins. Athletes respond to brief conversational surveys covering readiness, energy, effort, and injuries via SMS or WhatsApp. Coaches receive instant alerts and dashboards tracking team patterns.

No app downloads. No onboarding friction. No more athletes hiding injuries until it is too late. Just data you can act on, delivered where athletes already are.

Features

Core Platform Capabilities

Flag Alerts

Automatic notifications when athletes report low readiness, injury concerns, or high pain. Configurable thresholds.

Body Heatmap

18 anatomical regions visualized across your roster. Track where injuries cluster and how patterns shift.

Team Trends

Readiness, energy, and effort plotted over time. Identify peaks, valleys, and inflection points across a season.

AI Assistant

Natural language queries. "Who reported knee pain this week?" Get precise, data-backed answers in seconds.

Workout Log

Athletes log workouts and rehab via SMS/WhatsApp. Coaches track compliance without extra admin.

Leaderboards

Gamified fitness rankings drive engagement. Customizable metrics and display keep athletes accountable.

Player Drill-Down

Complete individual histories: readiness, injuries, workouts, and every survey response over time.

Custom Templates

Build sport-specific check-in surveys. Your questions, your scales, your coaching philosophy.
