01

What
Reflect
Does

Reflect automates post-practice and post-competition check-ins, delivering real-time athlete wellness data directly to coaches. Athletes respond to brief surveys covering readiness, energy, effort, and injuries.

Coaches receive instant alerts and dashboards tracking team patterns across weeks and months. The system works entirely through SMS and WhatsApp, meaning zero app installs and near-perfect compliance rates.
