# What is Reflect?

Reflect automates post-practice and post-competition check-ins, delivering real-time athlete wellness data to coaches. No app installs required — it works where athletes already are: SMS and WhatsApp.

Athletes respond to brief surveys covering readiness, energy, effort, and injuries. Coaches receive instant alerts and dashboards tracking team patterns across the entire season.

# Features

Automatic Flag Alerts

Real-time notifications for low readiness, injury concerns, high pain, energy-effort mismatches, and abnormal patterns. Configurable severity thresholds per metric. The system watches every check-in so you don't have to.

Integrates with Slack, email, and push notifications.

Body Heatmap

18 anatomical regions tracked per athlete. Visual overlay shows team-wide injury density.

Supports per-athlete and team-wide views.

Team Trends

Readiness, energy, and effort graphed over time. Spot fatigue cycles before they become injuries.

Exportable as CSV or PDF reports.

AI Assistant

Ask natural language questions: Who had low energy this week? Get data-backed answers instantly.

Powered by your team's real check-in data.

Custom Templates + Leaderboards

Build sport-specific, position-specific, or injury-specific check-in question sets. Gamified fitness tracking with leaderboards drives athlete engagement and accountability through friendly competition.

Templates support branching logic and conditional questions.

# How It Works

1

reflect init

Coach creates a team, imports the roster (CSV or manual entry), and sets check-in schedule. Takes under 5 minutes.

2

Automated Check-Ins Deploy

After each practice or game, athletes receive a brief SMS or WhatsApp survey. Readiness, energy, effort, and injury status — answered in 60 seconds.

3

Data Flows to Dashboard

Responses populate the coach dashboard in real time. Flag alerts trigger immediately for concerning responses. Body heatmap updates with every check-in.

4

Patterns Emerge

Team trends reveal fatigue cycles, injury patterns, and readiness fluctuations over weeks and months. AI assistant answers complex queries instantly.
