What Is Reflect

The Always-On
Assistant Coach

Reflect automates post-practice and post-competition check-ins through SMS and WhatsApp. Athletes answer quick surveys on readiness, energy, effort, and injuries. Coaches get instant alerts and dashboards tracking team patterns — no app download required.

Built to Win

Features That Perform

Flag Alerts

Instant notifications when an athlete reports low readiness, injury concern, or high pain. Never miss a warning sign again.

Body Heatmap

18-region anatomical injury map shows exactly where your team hurts. Spot patterns before they become season-ending problems.

Team Trends

Track readiness, energy, and effort across your entire roster over time. See the big picture at a glance.

AI Assistant

Ask questions in plain English. "Who's been fatigued this week?" Get answers instantly from your team's data.

Workout Logging

Athletes log workouts and rehab progress. Coaches track compliance and recovery timelines without spreadsheets.

Leaderboards
