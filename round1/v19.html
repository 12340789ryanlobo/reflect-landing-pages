What Is Reflect?

The always-on assistant coach

Reflect automates athlete wellness check-ins so you know how every player on your team feels -- physically and mentally -- after every practice and competition.

Athletes respond to brief SMS or WhatsApp surveys. No app download required. No friction. Coaches receive real-time alerts, dashboards, and AI-powered insights that make invisible patterns visible.
