What
Reflect

Reflect automates post-practice and post-competition check-ins, delivering real-time athlete wellness data directly to coaches. Athletes respond to brief surveys covering readiness, energy, effort, and injuries.

Zero app installs. Near-perfect compliance rates. Dashboards, heatmaps, trend analysis, and an AI assistant that answers complex questions about your team.

Features
that matter.

01

Flag Alerts

Automatic notifications for low readiness, injury concerns, elevated pain, and abnormal patterns. Never miss a warning sign again.

Configurable thresholds per sport, position, and individual athlete. Delivered to dashboard, email, and SMS.
02

Body Heatmap

18 anatomical regions tracked per athlete. Visual overlay shows team-wide injury density at a glance.

Filter by date range, position group, or individual to spot clustering patterns.
03

Team Trends

Readiness, energy, and effort graphed over time. Spot fatigue cycles before they become injuries.

Longitudinal analysis with custom date ranges and position-group filtering.
04

AI Assistant

Natural language queries against your team data. Ask anything and get instant, data-backed answers. Like having another coach on staff.

Powered by contextual AI trained on sports science patterns and your team's historical data.
05

Workout Log

Athletes log workouts and rehab. Track recovery timelines and return-to-play milestones.

Structured logging with compliance tracking and return-to-play protocols.
06

Leaderboards

Gamified fitness tracking keeps athletes engaged. Per-player drill-down for coaches.

Customizable metrics and ranking criteria. Privacy controls for sensitive data.
07

Custom Templates

Build question sets per sport, position, or injury type. Standardize data collection across your organization.

Template library with sport-specific presets and drag-and-drop editor.
