System Overview

What Is Reflect?

Reflect automates post-practice and post-competition check-ins via SMS and WhatsApp. Athletes respond to brief conversational surveys covering readiness, energy, effort, and injuries. Coaches receive instant alerts and dashboards that track team-wide patterns in real time.

Works where athletes already are. No app downloads. No onboarding overhead. No compliance friction. Just text and respond.
