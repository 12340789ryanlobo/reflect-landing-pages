What Is Reflect

The always-on assistant coach

Reflect works where athletes already are — SMS and WhatsApp. After every practice or competition, athletes receive a brief survey covering readiness, energy, effort, and injuries. Coaches get instant flag alerts and a real-time dashboard tracking the patterns that matter. Designed for teams of 15 to 60+ athletes who need data without friction.
