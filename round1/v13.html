What Is Reflect

A clinical-grade wellness monitoring platform built for sport

Reflect automates the collection of athlete self-report data through SMS and WhatsApp messaging. Coaches and sports medicine professionals receive instant flag alerts and access a comprehensive dashboard for tracking team wellness patterns. Designed for teams of 15 to 60+ athletes with near-perfect compliance rates.

Platform Capabilities

Comprehensive athlete monitoring tools

Automatic Flag Alerts

Real-time notifications triggered by low readiness scores, injury reports, elevated pain levels, or trend anomalies. Configurable thresholds per sport, position, and individual athlete. Delivered to dashboard, email, and SMS.

Injury Body Heatmap

18-region anatomical visualization of reported soreness across your team. Identify clustering patterns by body region.

Team Trends Dashboard

Longitudinal tracking of readiness, energy, and perceived effort. Filter by individual, position group, or date range.

AI Coaching Assistant

Natural language interface for querying team data. Ask complex questions and receive structured, data-backed responses.

Workout and Rehab Logging

Structured logging for workouts and rehabilitation protocols. Monitor compliance rates and return-to-play timelines.

Custom Question Templates

Create sport-specific and role-specific check-in templates. Standardize data collection across your organization.

Implementation

How Reflect integrates into your workflow

Configure

Import your athlete roster and customize check-in questionnaires to your sport and monitoring needs.

Collect

Athletes receive automated SMS or WhatsApp surveys post-activity. Average completion time: 60 seconds.

Monitor

Data populates your dashboard in real time. Flag alerts notify staff of concerning responses immediately.

Intervene

Use trend analysis, heatmaps, and the AI assistant to inform clinical decisions and training modifications.

Real-Time Monitoring

Flag alerts that keep you ahead of injuries

Reflect continuously monitors athlete responses and automatically flags concerning patterns. Alerts are categorized by severity and delivered in real time, so your staff can intervene before minor issues become major injuries.

Each flag includes contextual data: historical trends, comparison to team baselines, and recommended actions from the AI assistant.
