System Description

Reflect is an automated athlete wellness monitoring system designed for coaches managing rosters of 15 to 60+ athletes. The system delivers brief post-practice and post-competition surveys via SMS and WhatsApp—platforms athletes already use daily—eliminating the need for app installations or account creation.

Survey responses (readiness, energy, effort, pain location and severity) are processed in real time and surfaced through a coaching dashboard featuring trend analysis, automatic flag alerts, injury body heatmaps, and a natural language AI assistant.

“Check-ins that take 60 seconds. Insights that last a season.”

02

Feature Specifications

FEAT-001 / Alert System

Automatic Flag Alerts

Real-time notifications triggered by low readiness scores, injury reports, high pain ratings, or anomalous patterns. Configurable thresholds per team and per athlete.

FEAT-002 / Visualization

Injury Body Heatmap

Aggregated pain data mapped across 18 anatomical regions. Color-coded severity indicators. Historical overlay for trend identification.

FEAT-003 / Analytics

Team Trends Dashboard

Time-series visualization of readiness, energy, and effort scores. Filterable by date range, position group, and individual athlete.

FEAT-004 / Intelligence

AI Coaching Assistant

Natural language query interface. Processes questions like “Who had low energy this week?” and returns data-backed responses with full context.

FEAT-005 / Tracking

Workout & Rehab Logging

Athlete-reported workout sessions and rehab compliance tracking. Volume metrics and adherence percentages surfaced in coach dashboard.

FEAT-006 / Configuration

Custom Survey Templates

Fully configurable survey flows. Pre-built templates for common scenarios. Support for Likert scales, numeric, and free-text responses.
