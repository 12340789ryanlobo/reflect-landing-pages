What Reflect Does

Works where athletes already are

Reflect sends brief wellness surveys via SMS or WhatsApp after practices and competitions. Athletes respond in 60 seconds, and coaches receive a real-time dashboard with trends, alerts, and AI-powered insights.

  • No app download required for athletes
  • Surveys cover readiness, energy, effort, and pain
  • Automatic alerts for concerning responses
  • AI assistant answers natural language questions
  • Built for rosters of 15 to 60+ athletes
