What Is Reflect?

The always-on assistant coach

Reflect automates athlete wellness check-ins so coaches know exactly how their team is feeling — physically and mentally — after every practice and competition. Athletes reply to brief SMS or WhatsApp surveys; coaches get real-time alerts and dashboards.

No more guesswork. No more spreadsheets. No more athletes hiding injuries until it's too late.

Core Features

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Automatic Flag Alerts

Get instant notifications when an athlete reports low readiness, injury concerns, or high pain levels. Never miss a warning sign.

Team Trends Dashboard
