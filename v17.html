Lead Story

Check-Ins That Take 60 Seconds. Insights That Last a Season.

By the Reflect Editorial Board

Coaches managing 15 to 60 or more athletes face an impossible challenge: knowing how every player truly feels after each practice and competition. Traditional methods — clipboard surveys, casual check-ins, gut instinct — leave dangerous gaps. Injuries go unreported. Fatigue compounds silently. By the time a problem surfaces, it's often too late for prevention.

Enter Reflect, the athlete wellness platform that works where athletes already are. Through automated SMS and WhatsApp check-ins, Reflect delivers brief conversational surveys to every athlete on your roster, then translates their responses into real-time alerts, dashboards, and actionable coaching intelligence.

No app installation required. No training sessions. No onboarding friction. Athletes simply reply to a text message, and coaches receive a comprehensive view of team wellness within minutes.

"The always-on assistant coach — that's what Reflect is. It never forgets to check in, never misses a pattern, never takes a day off." Product Mission Statement, Reflect

The platform's suite of tools includes automatic flag alerts for concerning responses, an injury body heatmap spanning 18 anatomical regions, a team trends dashboard, AI-powered coaching queries, and comprehensive workout and rehab logging — all unified under a single, intuitive interface.

How It Works

  1. Add Your Roster Upload athlete names and phone numbers. Setup takes under 10 minutes.
  2. Schedule Check-Ins Configure when surveys go out — post-practice, game day, rest days.
  3. Athletes Respond Brief SMS/WhatsApp surveys covering readiness, energy, effort, and injuries.
  4. Insights Delivered Real-time alerts, dashboards, and AI analysis arrive instantly.
Monitoring

Automatic Flag Alerts

The system watches every incoming response for warning signs: low readiness scores, injury mentions, high pain ratings, or sudden drops in any metric. Coaches receive instant notifications when any athlete crosses a configurable threshold, ensuring no red flag goes unnoticed in a busy season.

Injury Body Heatmap

A visual overlay across 18 anatomical regions aggregates pain reports across your entire roster. See at a glance where your team hurts most, and track how injury patterns shift over weeks and months of training.

Analytics

Team Trends Dashboard

Readiness, energy, and effort plotted over time for your entire team. Identify when your squad is peaking, when they're grinding, and when they need rest. The dashboard surfaces patterns that are invisible in daily noise but critical over the arc of a full season.

Per-Player Drill-Down

Select any athlete and access their complete history: readiness trends, injury timeline, workout logs, and survey responses. Build a comprehensive picture of each individual's trajectory.

Intelligence

AI Coaching Assistant

Ask questions in natural language. "Which players have reported shoulder pain in the last two weeks?" "What's the team's average readiness after away games?" The AI assistant queries your entire data history and returns precise, actionable answers in seconds.

Custom Question Templates

Every sport is different. Build your own check-in surveys with sport-specific questions that matter to your program, your training philosophy, and your athletes.

Additional Features

Workout & Rehab Logging

Athletes log completed workouts and rehab exercises directly through the same SMS/WhatsApp channel. Coaches track compliance rates and see exactly who's doing the work — without nagging, without spreadsheets, without guesswork.

"We went from 30% survey completion to over 90% in the first week. It's just a text — there's nothing to download." Sarah K., Club Soccer Director

Fitness Leaderboards

Healthy competition drives engagement. Configurable leaderboards rank athletes by workout consistency, readiness scores, or custom metrics. Players see where they stand; coaches see who's showing up.
