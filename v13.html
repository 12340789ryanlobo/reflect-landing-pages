What Is Reflect

A clinical-grade wellness monitoring platform built for sport

Reflect automates the collection of athlete self-report data — readiness, energy, effort, and injury status — through SMS and WhatsApp messaging. Coaches and sports medicine professionals receive instant flag alerts and access a comprehensive dashboard for tracking team wellness patterns over time. Designed for teams of 15 to 60+ athletes.

Platform Capabilities

Comprehensive athlete monitoring tools

Automatic Flag Alerts

Real-time notifications triggered by low readiness scores, injury reports, elevated pain levels, or trend anomalies across your roster.

Injury Body Heatmap

18-region anatomical visualization of reported soreness and injury across your team. Identify clustering patterns by body region.

Team Trends Dashboard

Longitudinal tracking of readiness, energy, and perceived effort. Filter by individual, position group, or custom date ranges.

AI Coaching Assistant

Natural language interface for querying team data. Ask complex questions and receive structured, data-backed responses.

Workout & Rehab Logging

Structured logging for workouts and rehabilitation protocols. Monitor compliance rates and return-to-play timelines.

Custom Question Templates

Create sport-specific and role-specific check-in templates. Standardize data collection across your organization.

Implementation

How Reflect integrates into your workflow
