Getting Started

Up and running in minutes.

No technical setup required. Import your roster, set your schedule, and Reflect handles the rest.

1

Import Your Roster

Upload a CSV or add athletes manually. Name, phone number, sport. That's all we need.

2

Set Your Schedule

Tell Reflect when practices and games happen. Check-ins trigger automatically after each session.

3

Athletes Respond

Athletes get a text. They reply with numbers and short answers. Under 60 seconds, no app needed.

4

Coaches See Everything

Real-time dashboard with readiness scores, injury tracking, alerts, and AI-powered insights.
