How It Works

Three steps. Zero friction.

Set up in minutes. Start collecting insights today.

Athletes get a text

After practice or competition, Reflect sends a brief check-in via SMS or WhatsApp. Five questions, sixty seconds.

They reply instantly

No app, no login, no friction. Athletes respond with quick ratings for readiness, energy, effort, and any injuries.
