How It Works

Coaches see everything. Athletes do almost nothing.

Reflect sends brief wellness surveys after every practice and competition via SMS or WhatsApp. Athletes respond in under 60 seconds. Coaches get real-time dashboards with readiness scores, injury heatmaps, trend lines, and AI-powered insights. No app downloads. Zero friction.

  • SMS & WhatsApp — no app required
  • 5 questions, 60 seconds to complete
  • Instant flag alerts for coaches
  • AI-powered trend analysis
