Automated SMS & WhatsApp Check-Ins

Schedule post-practice and post-competition surveys that go out automatically. Athletes respond in under 40 seconds — no app download required.

Real-Time Dashboard

See your entire roster's wellness at a glance. Readiness scores, energy levels, and effort ratings update the moment athletes respond.

Flag Alerts

Instant notifications when an athlete reports pain, drops below baseline, or misses a check-in. Catch issues before they become injuries.

Injury Heatmaps

Visual body maps showing where your team reports discomfort over time. Spot patterns across positions, training loads, and seasons before they become sidelining injuries.

AI Coaching Insights

An AI assistant that analyzes trends across your roster and surfaces actionable recommendations — load adjustments, recovery suggestions, and return-to-play guidance.
