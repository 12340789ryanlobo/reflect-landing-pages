~*~ What Is Reflect? ~*~

Reflect automates post-practice and post-competition check-ins via SMS and WhatsApp, delivering real-time athlete wellness data to coaches!!

Athletes respond to brief surveys (readiness, energy, effort, injuries) and coaches receive INSTANT ALERTS and dashboards tracking team patterns. No app download needed. It just works!!
